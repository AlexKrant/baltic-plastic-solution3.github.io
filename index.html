Investment casting

Blanks for spare parts for power machines. Large-sized castings up to 720 mm in size

We manufacture high-precision castings with minimal allowances using equiaxed crystallization casting technology. We set up serial production of parts from casting and heat-resistant alloys, the mechanical processing of which is difficult.

We work with castings of complex configuration: large-sized, thin-walled, with grooves and internal cavities. The main area of ​​work is blanks of parts for gas turbine power machines: turbine blades, swirlers and other hot tract parts.

Complex configuration of parts

Castings with internal cavities and grooves. Obtaining units that would have to be assembled from separate parts during mechanical processing.

Large castings

Manufacturing of parts up to 720 mm in size.

Working with difficult to deform materials

Castings with special properties that are difficult to machine.

High precision castings

Parts with minimal allowances for machining. Possibility to exclude mechanical machining in some cases.
